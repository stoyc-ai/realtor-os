# Welcome to Your AI Real Estate Assistant
### Powered by STOYC · Built on Claude

You just added a full real estate team to your computer. Not another app to log into, not another dashboard to learn — an assistant you talk to in plain English that handles the busywork so you can focus on clients and closings.

This guide gets you up and running in a few minutes and shows you how to get the most out of it.

---

## What it does for you

Think of it as **five specialists working for you, all in one place:**

- **Your front desk** — drafts replies to every new lead in *your* voice, in seconds, so you never lose one to a slow response.
- **Your morning chief of staff** — every day it tells you who to call, what's urgent, and even pre-writes your follow-ups.
- **Your marketing department** — turns a new listing into a full set of posts, emails, and a landing page; writes your newsletters, blogs, and ads; manages your Google reviews; and audits your website so you show up on Google *and* in AI search.
- **Your research analyst** — builds CMAs, pulls market trends, and preps you to win listing appointments.
- **Your database manager** — wakes up your old, forgotten leads and keeps your past clients warm.

Everything it writes sounds like **you** — it learns your voice from your real posts and emails — and **nothing gets sent or changed without your approval.**

## Getting started (about 5 minutes)

We've already connected your CRM and set up your account, so most of the heavy lifting is done. To finish:

1. **Open the Claude desktop app** and go to **Cowork**.
2. **Pick a working folder** when asked — this is where your preferences and "voice" are saved so it remembers them.
3. Say: **"Onboard me"** — it'll walk you through teaching it your voice (just paste a few of your best posts/emails) and your basics (brokerage, areas, how you like to work).

That's it. From now on, you just talk to it.

## Connect your tools (the more you connect, the more it does)

The system gets more powerful the more of your tools it can see. Connect these in **Cowork → Settings → Connectors**. Your **CRM is already connected** by STOYC — these add the rest.

**Do these first (the essentials):**
- **Your CRM (Follow Up Boss)** — *already connected for you.* This powers your leads, follow-ups, morning briefings, call lists, and dashboards. It's the engine.
- **Email (Gmail or Outlook)** — so it can see your lead conversations and draft replies, follow-ups, and newsletters for you.
- **Calendar (Google Calendar)** — so your morning briefing and appointment prep know your schedule.
- **Google Drive** — so it can pull your past listings and posts to learn your voice.

**Turn on your marketing (connect the ones you actually use):**
- **Canva** — drop your social posts and just-listed graphics straight into your designs.
- **Mailchimp or Klaviyo** — send the newsletters and drip campaigns it writes.
- **Webflow or WordPress** — publish blog posts and listing pages it creates.
- **Gamma** — build listing-presentation decks.
- **Google Business Profile** — add STOYC as a **Manager** on your profile (we'll walk you through it) so it can post updates and reply to your Google reviews.

**Good to know:** it still works without these — if a tool isn't connected, it just hands you the finished content to copy in yourself. Connecting simply removes the copy-paste and lets it act for you (always with your approval).

## How to actually use it: just ask

There's nothing to "operate." You type or say what you want, like you'd text an assistant. A few to try on day one:

- **"Run my whole morning."** → your briefing, your call list, and drafted follow-ups, ready to approve.
- **"Who should I call today?"** → a ranked list with *why* and a ready opener for each.
- **"Reply to this lead."** (paste their message) → a reply in your voice.
- **"I just listed 123 Oak St — make all the marketing."** → description, social posts, email, landing page, and a video script.
- **"Get me ready to pitch the sellers at 14 Maple."** → a full CMA, pricing, and market story.
- **"Reactivate my old leads."** → it finds your cold contacts and drafts re-engagement messages.
- **"Audit my website."** → a plain-English report on how to get found on Google and AI.

Don't overthink the wording — say it however feels natural. If it needs more info, it'll ask.

Beyond quick tasks, you have **Agents** — they take on a *whole job* start to finish and hand you finished drafts:

- **Daily Command** — runs your entire morning (schedule it to run on its own).
- **New-Lead Responder** — researches a new lead, drafts the reply, sets the follow-up. Point it at *all* of today's leads at once.
- **Listing Launcher** — one address → your complete marketing rollout.
- **Listing Prep** — everything you need to win and price a listing appointment.
- **Reactivation Campaign** — works your entire cold database for you.

Just say the job ("work my newest leads," "launch this listing") and the right teammate takes over.

## Tips to get the most out of it

- **Teach it your voice once.** The more samples you give it, the more every post and email sounds like you. Say *"learn my voice."*
- **Tell it your rules.** Say things like *"I never text leads on Sundays"* or *"always follow up within an hour"* — it remembers, forever.
- **Be specific.** "Write a just-listed post for 123 Oak, 4 bed, with the pool" beats "write a post."
- **You're always in control.** It drafts; you review and send. Nothing goes out — no email, no post, no CRM change — without your okay.
- **Capture open houses instantly.** Snap a photo of your paper sign-in sheet and say *"add these to my CRM."*
